San Sebastian — 6-10 April 2026

Who: Jo, Scott, Tom & Sophie

Flights: Manchester to Bilbao (easyJet)

Accommodation: Airbnb (booked)

Transport: TBC (hire car or bus)

Day 1 — Monday 6 April (Arrive)

Travel

  • Fly Manchester to Bilbao (easyJet, ~2hrs)
  • Bilbao airport to San Sebastian (~1hr 15min by car/bus)

Afternoon/Evening

  • Check into Airbnb, settle in
  • Walk to La Concha beach — get bearings
  • Pintxos crawl #1 — Old Town (Parte Vieja)
    • Ganbara — mushrooms with egg yolk
    • Bar Txepetxa — anchovy creations
    • La Vina — famous Basque cheesecake

Day 2 — Tuesday 7 April (City & Coast)

Morning

  • Mount Urgull hike — easy 1-2hr walk from Old Town
  • Castle at the top, panoramic views over La Concha
  • Explore the old fortress and Jesus statue

Afternoon

  • La Concha beach — relax, walk the promenade
  • Wander Old Town streets
  • Optional: Aquarium or San Telmo Museum (Basque culture)

Evening

  • Pintxos crawl #2 — different bars
    • Bar Sport — baby squid stuffed with crab
    • Tamboril — best mushrooms
    • Bar Paco Bueno — fried shrimp, great value

Day 3 — Wednesday 8 April (Day Hike)

Option A: Flysch Route (Recommended)

  • Drive to Zumaia (~30 mins)
  • Coastal walk Zumaia to Deba along dramatic cliff formations
  • 8km, 3-4 hours, moderate difficulty
  • Spectacular geology — 60 million year old rock layers
  • Bus back to Zumaia or arrange pickup

Option B: San Sebastian to Zarautz

  • Camino del Norte section
  • Coastal path through Txakoli wine country
  • ~12km, 4-5 hours
  • End in Zarautz (surf town), bus back

Evening

  • Dinner in Zarautz or back in San Seb
  • Lighter pintxos night — you'll be tired!

Day 4 — Thursday 9 April (Culture Day Trip)

Option A: San Juan de Gaztelugatxe

  • 1hr 15min drive
  • The Game of Thrones island (Dragonstone)
  • 241 steps to chapel at top
  • Dramatic photos, worth the trip
  • Stop in Bermeo (fishing village) for lunch

Option B: Getaria & Wine Country

  • 30 min drive
  • Fishing village, birthplace of Balenciaga
  • Txakoli wine tasting at local bodega
  • Excellent seafood restaurants (grilled turbot)
  • Walk to Zarautz along coast if energy allows

Final Evening

  • Big pintxos crawl or book a proper restaurant
  • Restaurant options: Bodegon Alejandro (traditional), Kokotxa (Michelin star — book ahead)

Day 5 — Friday 10 April (Depart)

Morning

  • Final walk along La Concha
  • Coffee in Old Town
  • Pick up any gifts (Idiazabal cheese, Txakoli wine)

Midday

  • Drive to Bilbao airport (~1hr 15min)
  • Return car / catch bus
  • Fly Bilbao to Manchester
